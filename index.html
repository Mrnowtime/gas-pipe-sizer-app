Natural Gas and LPG sizing is based on NZ/AS 5601 (current edition) — always confirm final sizing using the process detailed in that standard. O₂ and N₂ sizing calculations are based on adapted hydraulic equations using published specific gravity values and are provided as a guide only — these gases are not covered by NZ/AS 5601 and sizing must be verified by a suitably qualified engineer before installation.
